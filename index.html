Biography:

My name is Nolan Duarte. I am a first-year student at RVCC. I am the youngest of three and I have two brothers. I don’t have any pets, but I do enjoy spending time with my brother’s dog and if I had one, it would probably be a golden retriever. In my free time, I like to play video games, watch movies/TV, and listen to music. One of the places I enjoy going to is Lake Champion, which hosts the camp that I go to called Camp Veritas, which is a Christian camp for ages 12-18. Thanks to Camp Veritas, I managed to meet many new people and make friends. I also have a bucket list, which contains some of the places that I want to visit such as London or Paris.

Hobbies:

Some of my hobbies are what I like to do in my spare time. I like to play video games such as PUBG Mobile and FC Mobile. I also play other games such as NBA games and Madden.
